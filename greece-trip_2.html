Naxos

Home base

The anchor: biggest, cheapest, most central of the Cyclades — long beaches, a Venetian old town, mountain villages, and the best local food on the route. Your launchpad for a day trip.

NAXOS · stay near Chora (town) or Agios Prokopios beach

See & do

  • PortaraGiant marble temple gate on the islet — sunset spot
  • Chora old town & KastroVenetian castle quarter, lanes of tavernas
  • Plaka & Agios Prokopios beachesLong, shallow, golden
  • Mountain villagesRent a car: Halki, kitron tastings, Apollonas kouros

    Crete

    Finale · Heraklion

    The big finish and your fly-out island. Base near Heraklion for the airport. Crete is the most varied island on the trip — palaces, hill villages, and some of Greece's best beaches — and far better value than Santorini.

    CRETE · base near Heraklion (HER) for the Aug 4 / 16:40 flight

    See & do

    • Palace of KnossosEurope's oldest city; the Minotaur's labyrinth
    • Archaeological MuseumThe Minoan frescoes from Knossos
    • Elounda & SpinalongaBoat to the fortress-island; turquoise swims
    • Heraklion old townVenetian Koules fortress, harbor dinners
